## 💡 CUSTOMIZATION EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Adding Author 2 Name

```latex

```

**After:**
```latex
Dr. Rajesh Kumar\IEEEauthorrefmark{2}
```

### Example 2: Adding Institution
**Before:**
```latex
University Name, City, Country
```

**After:**
```latex
Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, New Delhi, India
```
